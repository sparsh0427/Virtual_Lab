Demonstration and Implementation of cryptographic algorithms.

Procedure

The aim is to learn about various network devices in detail. To perform the experiment follow the below steps.

  1. Click On the Simulation tab from the left dropdown menu.
  2. The aim is to demonstrate and understand how different algorithms of cryptography works.
  3. The first algorithm shows how symeteric cryptography works.
  4. Then type the message(combination of any characters) and your secret key and press submit.
  5. Then in step 2 of that algorithm enter your secret key and press submit to see your message.
  6. The second algorithm gives you the basic understanding of how asymeteric cryptography works.
  7. Click on the Generate Keys button to generate your Random keys.
  8. Then type the message(combination of any characters) you want to encrypt along with the public key in the encryption key box and then press Encode message button.
  9. Then enter your private key in the description key box to see the decrypted message which would be the same as your original message
  10. Also try entering false private key and you will observe that the message is not deecrypted successfully and is different from your original message
  11. The third and the last algorithm is about a type of asymmeteric algorithm - RSA Algorithm
  12. Click on the generate prime numbers to generate random prime numbers.
  13. Then click on Generate N, PHI, E button to generate the respective values
  14. Click on Generate Keys button to generate your keys.
  15. Then in the next step enter the message ( numeric ) you want to encrypt.
  16. You will observe that on pressing the Encrypt/Decrypt the original message ( Message ) is same as the decrypted message.
